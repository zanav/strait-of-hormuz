STRAITS OF HORMUZ

◆ NAVAL TACTICAL DEFENSE — FICTIONAL SCENARIO ◆
You command the USS Nimitz carrier strike group in the Gulf. Protect commercial oil tankers transiting the strait while neutralizing coastal threats. Intercept incoming anti-ship missiles, strike launch sites with Tomahawks, and reposition your fleet for best firing solutions. Your destroyers auto-engage surface threats. Lose if your carrier sinks or 3 tankers are hit. Win by destroying all 6 coastal launch sites.
